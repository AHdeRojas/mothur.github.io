## Default settings

A [phylip-formatted distance
matrix](phylip-formatted_distance_matrix) must be inputted
for pcoa to be successful.

    mothur > pcoa(phylip=98_sq_phylip_amazon.dist)

or

    mothur > pcoa(phylip=98_lt_phylip_amazon.dist)
