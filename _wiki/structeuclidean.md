$$D_{Structeuclidean} = \sqrt{ \sum_{j=1}^{S_T}  \left ( S_{Aj} - S_{Bj} \right )^2 }$$

-   where $$S_{Aj}$$ is the abundance of sample A in the jth OTU.
-   where $$S_{Bj}$$ is the abundance of sample B in the jth OTU.
